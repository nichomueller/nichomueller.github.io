## Experience

The main focus of the program is the development of a comprehensive methodology for the solution of parameterized partial differential equations using reduced order models. My research mainly revolves around space-time reduction algorithms for computational fluid-dynamics problems, low-rank approximation theory for general tensors, finite element solvers on unfitted meshes, and high performance computing tools.
